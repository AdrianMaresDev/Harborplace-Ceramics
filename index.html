Our Story & Craft

Our journey began from our family home in Baltimore over 20 years ago, rooted in a cherished tradition of crafting beautiful, hand-made ceramics and pottery. Beginning as a bonding experience with loved ones, our craft has become a thriving business that honors our roots and craftsmanship. Each piece dedicates a combination of quality, technique, and personal touch to your home.

We celebrate the art of imperfection, making all of our creations just as unique as the families behind them. We invite you to explore our collection and find a piece that resonates with you.
